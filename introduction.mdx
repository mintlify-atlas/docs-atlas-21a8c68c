## What is TktPlz?

TktPlz is a modern ticket booking and event discovery platform supporting four event formats:

- **Seating events** — Cinema-style seat selection with real-time locking
- **Open-zone events** — Zone-based ticketing for concerts and festivals
- **Online events** — Virtual event links delivered with your ticket
- **Registration events** — Form-based sign-ups for fests, workshops, and college events

Whether you are a user looking for weekend plans or an organizer launching your next big show, TktPlz has everything you need in one place.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up with your email and OTP, or continue with Google — no password required for Google accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse events">
    Explore events by category (movies, concerts, fests, online shows) or search by city and date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select seats and pay">
    Pick your seats in real time, apply any coupons, and complete checkout via Razorpay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your QR ticket">
    Your QR-code ticket is delivered instantly. Show it at the venue for a quick scan-and-enter.
  </Step>
</Steps>
